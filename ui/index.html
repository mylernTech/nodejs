Services We Offer

Website Design

Website Development

Using design mockups from our design team at the Website Design stage or from another designer, we construct fully functional websites conforming to industry standards and best practices. During development, we strive keep scalability and stability in mind. Therefore, as your business grow, you can get in touch with us to keep your website evolving to meet your needs.

Website Security and Optimization

When working on a website, speed and security are our foremost concerns. Nothing breaks the user experience quite like a website that takes forever to load. And even worse is an insecure website. We perform extensive testing and double-check that everything looks and works as it should well before deploying to production. We ensure that they have fast load times and lag free interactions. Working with skilled pen-testers, we also ensure security adheres to industry standards to give our clients peace of mind.

Responsive Website Development

We at Mylern Tech believe that designing a responsive website is not an option - it is a necessity. As a standard, all websites we build are designed to work on any device, big or small. Whether that is an oversized desktop computer monitor, a laptop or devices with small screens such as smartphones and tablets, our timeless designs adapt beautifully to them all ensuring that your users and potential customers will be getting the best possible experience from your mobile friendly website.
